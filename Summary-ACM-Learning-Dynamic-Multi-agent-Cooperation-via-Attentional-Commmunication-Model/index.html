Introduction

Author of the paper: from China University of Geosciences and Institute of Automation: Chinese Academy of Sciences.

  • Proposed CAN(Cooperation-Aware Network) to calculate the relationships among agents (which was used to ascertain the routing) and distill the infos into the messeage.
  • ACM integrates CAN with RL framework which demonstrates the outstanding ability in collaboration.
  • Tested on both discrete and continuos environments.

Approach

Definition

Use Shared Parameters Partial Observable MarkovDecision Process(SP-POMDP) to describe the multi-agent problem.The model consisits of 10-tuple .
